ISN Neuroscience School, UNIMED, Ondo

Date: 11 - 16 August 2024

Venue: University of Medical Sciences, Ondo, Nigeria

Theme: Gene-environment interactions in neurological disorders: a contemporary update

The school aims to provide a rounded scholarly contemporary update on the interplay of genetic and environmental factors in the pathogenesis of neurological disorders. Lectures will be delivered by distinguished international and local scholars.

  • Prof Michael Aschner (Albert Einstein College of Medicine, USA)
  • Prof Dr Julia Bornhorst (University of Wuppertal, Germany)
  • Prof. Monica Maria Aranha Bastos (Albert Einstein College of Medicine, New York, USA and State University of Londrina, Brazil)
